Who I am and what I do

In my day job, I am currently employed by the State of Nebraska, I split my time between my current job of Exchange System Administrator and my dream job of Website Developer.

In my other job, I own my own company (NRTainment Images) and offer my services as website developer/photography/videographer for several entertainers and a recording studio. As a retired entertainer (I was a bellydancer for many years, travling across the U.S. performing, and house dancer in a Greek restaurant), I have marketed myself to entertainers, because I understand their unique requirements for websites, and social media.

Why I am taking this class:

I am taking this class for two reasons:

  • I wish to learn more about dynamic web applications in order to apply that knowledge in my job
  • This course fulfills the requirement for the Web Development Certificate, and the Masters Program
